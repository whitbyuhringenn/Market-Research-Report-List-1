<p><ul><li>Pressing Bushing Type</li><li>Floating Bushing Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive kingpin market is categorized into two main types: pressing bushing type and floating bushing type. The pressing bushing type features a fixed bushing that is directly pressed into the steering knuckle, providing stability and durability. In contrast, the floating bushing type utilizes a bushing that can move slightly within the knuckle, allowing for better alignment and smoother steering dynamics. Both types serve essential roles in vehicle steering systems, affecting handling, longevity, and overall performance.</p></p>
