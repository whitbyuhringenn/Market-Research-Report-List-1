<p><p>Integrated Passive Devices (IPD) are miniaturized components that integrate passive elements like resistors, capacitors, and inductors on a single chip, enhancing circuit performance and reducing assembly complexity. The market for IPDs can be segmented into silicon and non-silicon types. Silicon IPDs leverage semiconductor technology for high integration and scalability, commonly used in RF and mixed-signal applications. Conversely, non-silicon IPDs, which can be made from ceramic or polymer materials, are typically used in specialized applications requiring unique characteristics like high temperature tolerance or specific dielectric properties.</p></p>
