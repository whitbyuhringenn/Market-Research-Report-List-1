<p><strong>The Industrial Robots Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Articulated Robots</li><li>Parallel Robots</li><li>SCARA Robots</li><li>Cylindrical Robots</li><li>Cartesian Robots</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial robots market comprises various types suited for specific applications. Articulated robots feature rotating joints for flexibility, making them ideal for tasks like welding and assembly. Parallel robots have a fixed base with moving arms, offering high precision for packaging and medical applications. SCARA robots excel in assembly tasks due to their rigid structure and horizontal motion. Cylindrical robots combine rotational and linear movements, appropriate for assembly lines. Cartesian robots, with three linear axes, are versatile for pick-and-place operations.</p></p>
