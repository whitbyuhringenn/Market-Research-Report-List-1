<p><strong>The Printing Inks for Flexible Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li><li>UV-curable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexible packaging printing inks market includes three main types: water-based, solvent-based, and UV-curable inks. Water-based inks are environmentally friendly, using water as a solvent, ideal for food packaging. Solvent-based inks contain organic solvents, offering robust adhesion and vivid colors, suitable for various substrates but less eco-friendly. UV-curable inks utilize ultraviolet light to cure the ink instantly, providing high durability and vibrant prints, making them popular for high-speed printing applications. Each type serves different needs and applications in flexible packaging.</p></p>
