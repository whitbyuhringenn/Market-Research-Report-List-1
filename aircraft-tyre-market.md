<p><strong>The Aircraft Tyre Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Main Tyre</li><li>Front Tyre</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the aircraft tyre market, two primary types are identified: main tyres and front tyres. Main tyres are designed for the main landing gears, supporting the majority of the aircraft's weight during takeoff, landing, and ground movements. They are engineered for durability and high performance. Front tyres, typically located on the nose gear, provide steering control and support the aircraft's front weight during taxiing and landings. Both types are critical for safe operations, emphasizing different performance characteristics suited to their specific roles.</p></p>
