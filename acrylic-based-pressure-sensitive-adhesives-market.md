<p><p>The acrylic-based pressure-sensitive adhesives (PSAs) market is divided into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based PSAs use organic solvents to dissolve acrylic polymers, providing strong adhesion and durability, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, water-based PSAs utilize water as the primary solvent, offering a more environmentally friendly and user-safe alternative. While water-based adhesives may have slightly lower performance in terms of adhesion strength compared to solvent-based options, they are increasingly favored due to regulatory compliance and sustainability concerns.</p></p>
