<p><ul><li>Anion Ion-exchange Polymer</li><li>Cation Ion-exchange Polymer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ion-exchange polymer market is primarily categorized into two types: anion ion-exchange polymers and cation ion-exchange polymers. Anion ion-exchange polymers are designed to exchange negatively charged ions, making them suitable for applications like water purification and chemical processing. Conversely, cation ion-exchange polymers focus on exchanging positively charged ions, often utilized in softening hard water and metal ion removal. Both types are essential in various industries, including water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food processing, serving critical functions in ion separation and purification processes.</p></p>
