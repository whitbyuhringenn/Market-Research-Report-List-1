<p><p>Metal Powder Additive Manufacturing encompasses various techniques, primarily Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM). SLM utilizes focused laser beams to selectively fuse layers of metal powder, allowing for intricate designs and high precision. EBM employs an electron beam in a vacuum to melt the powder, suitable for high-density materials and thicker layers. Other market types may include binder jetting and metal extrusion, each offering unique advantages for different applications, materials, and production scales.</p></p>
