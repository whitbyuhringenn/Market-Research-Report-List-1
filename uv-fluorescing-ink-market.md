<p><p>UV fluorescing ink is utilized in various printing applications, available as solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based inks are known for their strong adhesion and durability, making them suitable for outdoor and challenging environments. They often provide vibrant colors and faster drying times. In contrast, water-based inks are eco-friendly, offering lower volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and a safer option for indoor use. Both types are designed to enhance visibility under UV light, catering to diverse industrial and commercial needs.</p></p>
