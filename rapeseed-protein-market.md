<p><p>The rapeseed protein market is categorized mainly into two types: hydrolyzed rapeseed protein and rapeseed protein isolate. Hydrolyzed rapeseed protein is created through the enzymatic breakdown of protein, enhancing its solubility and digestibility, making it suitable for sports nutrition and functional foods. On the other hand, rapeseed protein isolate is a concentrated form, containing high protein levels, that is used in various food applications for its emulsifying and thickening properties. Both types cater to the growing demand for plant-based protein alternatives.</p></p>
