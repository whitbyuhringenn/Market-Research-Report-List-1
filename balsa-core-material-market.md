<p><p>The Balsa Core Material market is categorized into two main types: monolayer and multilayer. Monolayer balsa cores consist of a single layer of balsa wood, offering lightweight properties and good strength for various applications. In contrast, multilayer balsa cores comprise multiple layers of balsa wood bonded together, enhancing structural integrity and improving durability. These materials are widely utilized in aerospace, marine, and construction industries, as they provide superior stiffness-to-weight ratios, making them ideal for performance-driven projects.</p></p>
