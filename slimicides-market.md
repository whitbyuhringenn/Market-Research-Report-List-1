<p><p>The slimicides market is categorized into two main types: non-oxidizing and oxidizing. Non-oxidizing slimicides are chemical agents that inhibit the growth of microorganisms without altering their chemical structure, making them effective in various industrial applications. In contrast, oxidizing slimicides work by producing reactive oxygen species, which lead to the destruction of microbial cells. Each type serves specific needs in industries like paper, oil, and water treatment, ensuring efficient control of slime and biofilm formation.</p></p>
